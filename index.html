Brewing the Perfect Cup

Scroll through the full journey from roasted bean to silky latte art.

Start the journey Scroll to explore

Step 1 · Roasted Beans

Every cup starts here. Freshly roasted beans carry aromas of cocoa, caramel, and toasted nuts. Time and temperature determine whether your coffee is bright and fruity or deep and chocolatey.

As you scroll, the background slowly shifts from dark to warm tones, echoing the roasting process.

Step 2 · The First Pour

Hot water extracts flavour and oils from the grounds, creating a bold cup of black coffee. Acidity, sweetness, and bitterness all balance in this simple brew.

Hover over the cup to see it lift gently, as if being served to you.

Step 3 · Chocolate Mocha

Add rich cocoa, a touch of milk, and a smooth espresso shot to build a comforting mocha. The flavours layer like the patterns in the foam.

Steam rises from the cup using soft looping animations.

Step 4 · Latte Art Finale

Finally, perfectly textured milk meets espresso. Precise pours form layered petals of latte art – the signature of a practiced barista.

The latte gently pulses, highlighting the art without being distracting.
